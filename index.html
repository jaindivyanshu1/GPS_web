About Our School

Our Mission

Garment's Public School, established on August 5, 1995, by the Readymade Vastra Vyapari Parmarthik Trust, has been a cornerstone of quality education for nearly three decades. Located in the serene surroundings of Khanwa Naka, Indore, the school provides an ideal environment for holistic learning. The campus is nestled amidst abundant greenery, offering a safe, aesthetic, and nurturing space for every knowledge seeker.

At Garment's Public School, we are committed to fostering excellence in education while instilling strong moral values and discipline. Our curriculum is designed to cater to the all-round development of students, blending academic rigor with technical and life skills education. We believe in preparing students to meet the challenges of the future with confidence, competence, and character.

Our Vision and Mission

Our mission is to provide an inclusive and dynamic learning environment where students from diverse backgrounds can excel. We aim to nurture creativity, critical thinking, and ethical values in every learner, enabling them to achieve their full potential.
